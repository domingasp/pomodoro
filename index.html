Pomodoro Timer

Timer based on the Pomodoro Technique. This concept is basic and works in the following way. Each 'Pomodoro' is 25 minutes in which work is done, each 'Pomodoro' is followed by a 5 minute rest. Once 4 'Pomodoros' have been reached the rest period is 20 minutes before the process restarts again.
